📝 How to Add Categories

When writing an article, add this to the top of your file (called "front matter"):

---
title: "Your Article Title"
date: 2026-04-14
categories: [Technology, Reviews, Lifestyle]
highlight: false
---

Then articles will automatically appear under these categories!

💡 Popular Category Ideas

  • Technology - Gadgets, software, tech news
  • Reviews - Product and service reviews
  • Lifestyle - Life tips, productivity, wellness
  • Personal - Personal stories and experiences
  • Opinion - Editorials and thought pieces
