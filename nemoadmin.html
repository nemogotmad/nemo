📋 How to Write an Article

  1. Click "Create New Article" above
  2. Name your file: YYYY-MM-DD-title-with-dashes.md (example: 2026-04-14-my-first-article.md)
  3. Copy this template into the file:
---
title: "Your Article Title Here"
date: 2026-04-14
highlight: false
categories: [Category1, Category2]
---

> [!IMPORTANT]
> This is an important point you want to highlight!

Your article content goes here. Use **Markdown** for formatting.

## Subheading

More content here...

- Bullet points
- Lists
- [Links](https://example.com)

⭐ How to Highlight an Article

Change highlight: false to highlight: true in the article's front matter. Highlighted articles appear in the Featured section on the homepage.

📂 How to Add Categories

In the front matter, change categories: [Category1, Category2] to your desired categories.

💰 How to Add Ads in Your Articles

Paste this code anywhere in your article:

    <div class="ad-label">Sponsored</div>
    <div class="ad-content">
